Thai Prawn Salad

This vibrant Thai prawn salad is a refreshing and flavorful dish that perfectly balances sweet, sour, salty, and spicy notes.Every bite offers a delightful mix of textures and a truly authentic taste of Thai cuisine.

(Boiled prawn,lettuce,mango,red cabbage,tomato,dressed with sweet & spicy thai sauce,topped with peanut).

Signature Beef Steak

This exquisite beef steak features a tenderloin/sirloin cut, grilled to perfection for a juicy and flavorful experience. Served alongside a medley of sauted vegetables (green beans, carrots, and cauliflower) delicately cooked with garlic and onion to enhance their natural flavors. A rich and creamy mushroom sauce is served on the side, adding depth and smoothness to the dish. Finished with a touch of coral pink garnish, this plate is a harmonious blend of taste, texture, and elegant presentation.

(Grill meltique beef TENDERLOIN/SIRLOIN,serve with mix vegetable,mash potato and cream mushroom sauce).
